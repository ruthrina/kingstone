Admissions

How to Join Kingstone High School

We welcome prospective students and their families to become part of the Kingstone High School community. Follow the steps below to complete your admission process.

Step 1: Application

Pick up an application form from the school office or download it from our website. Complete and submit the form with all required documents.

Step 2: Interview & Assessment

Attend the scheduled interview and placement tests for students to assess readiness and placement in the appropriate grade.

Step 3: Admission Decision

Receive the admission decision via email or phone. Once admitted, proceed with tuition payment and other registration formalities.

Required Documents

Documents for Admission

Birth Certificate

A copy of the applicant's birth certificate is required for verification purposes.

Academic Records

Submit recent report cards or transcripts from the previous school attended.

Passport Photos

Provide two recent passport-sized photographs of the applicant.
